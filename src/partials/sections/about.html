About LKT

The Lykke Utility Token is an ERC20-based token that confers the right to obtain and pay for future services within the Lykke Ecosystem:

Professional services

  • A variety of data services: streaming market data and historical outlook data, consumer data from investing to spending habits, and rewards or payments to clients for agreeing to share personal data
  • Country-specific tax reports
  • Collateral for margin trading or peer-to-peer lending
  • Channel collateral
  • Listing fees paid by the issuer
  • Custody fees paid by the user
  • Liquidity fees
  • POS merchant fees
  • Preferential access to ICOs

Rebates/discounts

  • Discounted exchange fees for professional traders
  • Reduced fees when investing in LKK liquidity
  • Reduced asset management fees
  • Reduced fees for peer-to-peer lending
  • Rewards for new quant/algo models
